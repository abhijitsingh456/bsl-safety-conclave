About Us

Who We Are

The Safety Conclave 2025 is being organized by the Safety Engineering Department of SAIL Bokaro Steel Plant. As a key unit of the Steel Authority of India Limited (SAIL), Bokaro Steel Plant is committed to promoting safety, innovation, and operational excellence in the steel industry.

Our department has long championed a proactive approach to workplace safety and health, implementing advanced protocols and fostering a culture of continuous improvement and responsibility.

Why "अरिष्ट"

"अरिष्ट" (Arisht), meaning 'without harm', symbolizes our vision for a future with Zero Harm. It reflects our mission to embrace digital innovation and transformative technologies that can enhance safety practices across integrated steel plants.

This conclave serves as a collaborative platform to share ideas, showcase advancements, and align industry efforts with the national goal of a Viksit Bharat. Together, we strive to set new benchmarks in safety excellence.
